In this project we're going to be looking at cities and how they came to be. These mega-structures made mainly by concrete populate most of earth now. They are all unique and no one city is the same, since each city reflects it's people and their heritage and everything they stand for.

Age of Skyscrapers

Our cities to this day are characterized by more than one Skyscrapers. It has become a symbol for modern day cities. It has reached a point where countries and cities alike are competing to hold the title of home to the tallest building on earth.

Another City Underground

For most of our cities whatever infrastructure is needed to run it mostly goes invisible. Deep down under the towers and the concrete jungle exists a whole other city underneath filled with tunnels and even more tunnels that supply water, gas and what runs the world today, the internet. The tunnels also carry away waste to areas far away from the city and without this bedrock of tunnels our cities would just be a pile of mess.
